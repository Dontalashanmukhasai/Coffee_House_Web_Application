What's make our coffee special!

At our coffee shop, what sets us apart is our commitment to quality and craftsmanship in every cup. We source the finest coffee beans from sustainable farms, ensuring freshness and robust flavors in every brew. Our baristas are passionate about their craft, meticulously crafting each drink to perfection. Whether you prefer a rich espresso, a velvety latte, or a refreshing cold brew, every sip at our coffee shop promises a delightful experience that lingers with you long after the last drop.

Our commitment to quality extends beyond the cup. We believe in sustainable practices and supporting the communities where our coffee is grown. That's why we partner with farmers who prioritize environmental stewardship and fair trade. Cold Brew

Coffee that is brewed with cold water over a longer period, typically 12-24 hours. It results in a smooth, less acidic coffee that is served cold or over ice.

Mocha

A coffee drink that combines espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup or cocoa powder. It has a sweet, chocolatey flavor and is often topped with whipped cream.

Americano

A coffee drink prepared by diluting espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to, but different flavor from, regular drip coffee.

Macchiato

An espresso with a small amount of steamed milk added. It can be served either as a "caffe macchiato" (espresso marked with a little milk) or a "latte macchiato" (milk "stained" with espresso).

Arabica(seeds)

Flavor: Known for its smooth, complex flavor and aromatic properties. Caffeine Content: Lower than Robusta. Growing Conditions: Prefers higher altitudes (600-2,200 meters), cooler climates. Origin: Ethiopia. Varieties: Includes Typica, Bourbon, SL28, SL34, Gesha, and more.

₹95

Robusta (Coffea canephora)

Flavor: Stronger, harsher taste with a grainy or nutty flavor and a higher bitterness. Caffeine Content: Higher than Arabica. Growing Conditions: Tolerates lower altitudes (sea level to 800 meters) and warmer climates. Origin: Central and Western Africa. Varieties: Includes Conilon, and several hybrids like Nescafe's Nescafé Plan varieties.

₹95

Liberica (Coffea liberica)

Flavor: Unique, bold, smoky flavor with a fruity and floral aroma. Caffeine Content: Lower than Robusta but higher than Arabica. Growing Conditions: Grows in low altitudes and humid environments. Origin: Liberia, West Africa.

₹95

Excelsa (Coffea excelsa)

Flavor: Tart, fruity, and complex, often used in blends to add depth. Caffeine Content: Similar to Liberica. Growing Conditions: Grows in similar conditions as Liberica, often in Southeast Asia. Origin: Reclassified as a variety of Liberica but often distinguished for its unique taste.

₹95
