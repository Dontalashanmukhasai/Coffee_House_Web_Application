Espresso & Coffee

A concentrated coffee brewed by forcing a small amount of nearly boiling water through finely-ground coffee beans. It has a strong flavor and a rich, creamy layer on top called crema.

Cappuccino

Cappuccinos are known for their frothy texture and often have a dusting of cocoa powder or cinnamon on top.

Cold Brew

Coffee that is brewed with cold water over a longer period, typically 12-24 hours. It results in a smooth, less acidic coffee that is served cold or over ice.

₹99

Mocha

A coffee drink that combines espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup or cocoa powder. It has a sweet, chocolatey flavor and is often topped with whipped cream.

₹100

Americano

A coffee drink prepared by diluting espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to, but different flavor from, regular drip coffee.

₹99

Macchiato

An espresso with a small amount of steamed milk added. It can be served either as a "caffe macchiato" (espresso marked with a little milk) or a "latte macchiato" (milk "stained" with espresso).
